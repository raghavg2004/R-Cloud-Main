🚀 TeleDrive Setup

5-minute Telegram file manager setup

1Get Your Bot Token

What you need: A Telegram bot token from @BotFather

  1. Open Telegram
  2. Search for @BotFather
  3. Send /newbot
  4. Choose a name (e.g., "TeleDrive")
  5. Choose a username (must be unique, e.g., "teledrive_storage_bot")
  6. Copy the token that appears
2Create Private Storage Channel

What you need: A private Telegram channel

  1. Open Telegram
  2. Go to Menu → New Channel
  3. Enter channel name: "TeleDrive Storage"
  4. Choose Private Channel
  5. Skip adding members
  6. Create the channel

Important: The channel MUST be private!

Next, we'll add your bot to this channel
3Add Bot to Channel

What you need: Add your bot as admin to the channel

  1. Open your private channel in Telegram
  2. Go to Info (or swipe right)
  3. Scroll down and select Administrators
  4. Tap Add Administrator
  5. Search for your bot username
  6. Select it and give permissions:
    • ✓ Post Messages
    • ✓ Delete Messages
  7. Save changes
Your bot MUST be admin to upload files!
4Get Your Channel ID

What you need: Your channel's unique ID

  1. Open your private channel in Telegram
  2. Forward ANY message from the channel to @userinfobot
  3. Look for "Channel ID" or "Supergroup ID"
  4. Copy the entire number (starts with -100)

Example format:

-1001234567890
5Configure TeleDrive

Final step: Enter your credentials in TeleDrive app

  1. Open index.html in your browser
  2. Click ⚙️ Settings (top-right)
  3. Paste your credentials:
    • Bot Token: •••••
    • Channel ID: •••••
    • API Server URL: Leave empty
  4. Click "Test Connection"
  5. If it says ✓ Connected, click "Save Settings"
That's it! You're all set!
